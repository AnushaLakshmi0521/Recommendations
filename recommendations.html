What Our System Does

Our solution combines soil analytics, crop physiology, environmental intelligence, and machine learning to deliver real-time farm insights and actionable recommendations. The system analyzes crop health, soil condition, crop stage, nutrient balance, and other metrics then converts raw field data into precision agronomy guidance and gives suggestions to improve the crop health and therefore the improved productivity.

Plant Health Assessment

The system evaluates crop growth, pest risk, nutrient imbalance, irrigation condition, and environmental response to generate a Plant Health Index (PHI). It identifies early-stage issues and provides clear alerts before yield loss occurs.

Precision Recommendation System

Our system analyzes nutrient gaps, stress factors, and crop requirements to deliver a targeted treatment strategy. It recommends the right fertilizer, pesticide, or micronutrient along with the appropriate dosage and timing based on the crop stage and field conditions.
